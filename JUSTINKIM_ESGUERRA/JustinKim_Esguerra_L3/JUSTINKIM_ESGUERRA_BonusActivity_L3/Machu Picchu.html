Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) peak. It is situated on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain ridge above the Sacred Valley, which is 80 kilometers (50 miles) northwest of Cusco and through which the Urubamba River flows.
