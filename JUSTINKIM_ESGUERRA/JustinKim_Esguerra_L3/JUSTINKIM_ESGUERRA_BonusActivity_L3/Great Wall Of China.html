Great Wall Of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications that stretch across northern China, built to protect against invasions and raids. It is one of the most iconic structures in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage site. The wall was constructed over several dynasties, with the earliest sections dating back to the 7th century BC.
